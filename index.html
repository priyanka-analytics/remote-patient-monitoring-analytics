Remote Patient Monitoring Analytics

A Case Study on Heart Failure Management

Visualizing the impact of telemedicine through data and usability analysis.

Project Summary

This case study evaluates the role of remote patient monitoring in heart failure care. We tracked patient data, monitored usability of telemedicine systems, and identified challenges through interviews and contextual research. The project revealed improved outcomes and reduced hospital readmissions.
